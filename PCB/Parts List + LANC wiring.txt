LANC Wiring:

15 Pin Multiport:
Pin 1  Power ON/OFF
Pin 2  Ground		-> PCB Ground (G)
Pin 3  Video
Pin 4  Shutter
Pin 5  Focus
Pin 6  Select		-> PCB Select (S)
Pin 7  UART RX		-> PCB Transmit (T)
Pin 8  UART TX		-> PCB Receive (R)
Pin 9  XReset
Pin 10 3.3v

2.5mm Jack:		
Sleeve - Ground		-> PCB Ground (G)
Ring - Not connected
Tip - LANC Data		-> PCB LANC (L)
